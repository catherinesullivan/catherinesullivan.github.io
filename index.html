Siena College Programming Contest

The Siena College High School Programming Contest brings talented students from high schools throughout the Albany area to the campus. Contestants participate in a competition which will test their programming skills and problem solving abilities. Each high school is allowed to send one team. Contestants must be students currently attending that high school. Contests are generally held every March. And of course, there is tons of pizza!

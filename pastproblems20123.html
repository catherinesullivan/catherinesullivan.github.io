Program 3: 4 is my favorite number

Background Information: The author of this problem states that the number 4 is his favorite number.

Given two integers x and y, your program will find the sum of all integers, between x and y (inclusive), whose digits exactly sum up to the number four. The numbers 4, 121, 103 and 4000 are valid numbers for a summation, while 41, 5364, and 255 are not.

Programming Problem:

Input: Two positive integers x and y, where y ≤ 1 billion, and where x ≤ y.
Output: The sum of the numbers z on the interval [x, y] such that the digits of z add up exactly to the number 4.

Example 1: Input: 4 9
Output: 4

Example 2: Input: 7 237
Output: 1205

Example 3: Input: 57 186340292
Output: 14333333200

Example 4: Input: 57 186340292
Output: 0
